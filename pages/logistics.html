Logistics

An Auction System for Logistics in Ruby on Rails.


Logistics is a Rails Applications that is targeted for companies in need of a secure and easy Auction system. What this app does differently is that we essentially remove the need for a freight broker and include everything he does in the admin panel. Independent freight transporation can bid on contracts for delivery and they're able to see distance, weather, and current contract price.







Ruby Gems
- Rails 4
- Geoencode
- Maps4Rails
- time_diff
- OpenWeather
